---

VoucherPay lets a merchant sell a product or service through a card checkout while VoucherPay handles the payment session, card-entry handoff, voucher issuance, settlement tracking, and payout workflow.

The merchant keeps the normal store experience:

1. Customer adds an item to cart.
2. Your backend creates a VoucherPay checkout session.
3. Your frontend mounts the VoucherPay widget.
4. The customer reviews the order, accepts terms when required, enters card details, and completes 3DS if requested.
5. VoucherPay confirms capture, issues the voucher, sends webhooks, and shows the transaction in the merchant portal.

Use these docs if you are:

- integrating VoucherPay into an ecommerce store;
- testing the demo checkout flow;
- issuing API keys and webhooks from the merchant portal;
- reconciling captured payments, vouchers, withdrawals, and refunds.

## Current integration model

VoucherPay is designed around a hosted payment flow. Card details are entered into the acquiring provider's hosted card-entry surface, not into your store backend. Your system receives only VoucherPay session state, voucher references, and webhook events.
