---
title: "Checkout flow"
description: "The customer journey from your cart to VoucherPay success and merchant redirect."
---

A complete VoucherPay checkout should feel like part of your store:

1. Customer lands on your site.
2. Customer adds an item to cart and clicks checkout.
3. Your backend creates a VoucherPay checkout session with the order amount and reference.
4. Your frontend mounts the VoucherPay widget with the `sessionId` and `clientSecret`.
5. VoucherPay shows the merchant and order context.
6. If terms are required, the customer reviews and accepts them.
7. The hosted card-entry form loads.
8. Customer enters card details.
9. 3DS is completed when requested.
10. VoucherPay polls status using the browser-safe client secret.
11. On capture, VoucherPay shows success, issues the voucher, sends webhooks, and redirects the customer back to your store.

## Merchant identification

VoucherPay identifies the merchant at checkout-session creation. That is the key control: the order is tied to your merchant account before the cardholder reaches card entry or 3DS.

Your store must never create a generic payment first and decide the merchant later.

## Redirects

Provide HTTPS redirect URLs for success, failure, cancel, and in-progress states. VoucherPay validates redirect origins against your configured merchant origins.

For local development over plain `http://localhost`, the demo store omits redirects so the hosted card surface can render without browser security issues.
